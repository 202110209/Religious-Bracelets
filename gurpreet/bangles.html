Bangles and Churra

About Bangles

Bangles are traditional ornaments worn by women in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh. They are usually made of metal, glass, or plastic and come in various designs and colors.

About Churra

Churra is a type of bangle worn by Sikh women as a symbol of marriage. It is usually made of ivory, metal, or plastic and is worn in multiples on both wrists.
